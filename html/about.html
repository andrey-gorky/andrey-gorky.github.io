ALINA HAMALIA

Ukrainian artist. The philosophy of her work and the main idea in creating paintings is intuition. Various spiritual practices are used to achieve intent. First of all, he is interested in creating paintings that transform consciousness, harmonize the state of mind and heal the deepest wounds of the soul.

Directions in painting: marine, abstract, psychotronic art, digital art, VR | AR
